Fish Tacos

Recipe Description

Fried white fish, a creamy cilantro sauce, and toppings of your choice come together in this delicious taco recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.5lb white flaky fish of your choice
  • 1tsp coriander
  • 1tsp cumin
  • 1/2tsp chili powder
  • 1/2tsp paprika
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1.5cups all purpose-flour
  • 0.5cups cornstarch
  • fresh cilantro
  • 1cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 jalapeno, sliced lengthwise and chopped, innards and seeds removed
  • olive oil
  • shredded purple cabbage
  • cotija cheese
  • small flour tortillas
  • salsa (optional)

Steps

  1. slice fish into ~1/2in wide strips
  2. combine flour, cornstarch, coriander, cumin, childi powder, paprika, and salt in bowl. Whisk until completely combined.
  3. prepare a large plate with a paper towel over the top
  4. toss fish strips in the flour mixture, then place on the paper towel plate
  5. in a large frying pan, heat 2tbsp olive oil over medium-high heat
  6. once the oil is shimmering, add the fish strips and cook, approximately 2 minutes each side
  7. set cooked fish aside on a fresh paper towel
  8. add 1cup cilantro, the yogurt, avocado, jalapeno, and 2tbsp olive oil to a blender or food processor
  9. blend cilantro sauce until smooth in texture (density should be similar to ketchup)
  10. wrap 3-5 tortillas at a time in paper towel, then place on a plate and microwave for 20 seconds to warm
  11. to construct taco, add cabbage, then 2 fish strips, followed by sauce and other toppings of choice
