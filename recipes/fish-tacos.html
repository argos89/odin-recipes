Fish Tacos

Recipe Description

Fried white fish, a creamy cilantro sauce, and toppings of your choice come together in this delicious taco recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.5lb white flaky fish of your choice
  • 1tsp coriander
  • 1tsp cumin
  • 1/2tsp chili powder
  • 1/2tsp paprika
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1.5cups all purpose-flour
  • 0.5cups cornstarch
  • fresh cilantro
  • 1cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 jalapeno
  • olive oil
  • shredded purple cabbage
  • cotija cheese
  • salsa (optional)
