Deviled Eggs

Description

A perfect deviled egg is the ideal party appetizer. This recipe walks you through how to perfectly hard boil an egg, how to make the filling, and what toppings go best on a deviled egg.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 6cups water
  • mayonnaise
  • 1/4tsp salt
  • 1tsp dijon mustard
  • smoked paprika, to taste
  • chopped green onions (optional)
